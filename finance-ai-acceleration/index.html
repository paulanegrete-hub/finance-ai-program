01
What is this?

A hands-on program
designed to embed an AI-first mindset.

How it works

Prep work done in advance. Then two focused build days per country — in person, with real data, shipping real automations.

The goal

Stop experimenting with AI. Start operating with it — embedded in daily Finance workflows, driving measurable impact.

The outcome

A Finance team that builds fast, learns fast, and shapes the future — instead of reacting to it.

Strategic rationale — why now

As volume, speed, and regional scope expand, capacity remains tied to manual effort and headcount. This program exists to decouple capacity from headcount by scaling with intelligence, systems, and shared knowledge.
